Our Motivation

This tool is built by volunteers, for volunteers. We believe coaching resources should be accessible. All we ask is that this tool remains non-profit: no fees for yourself, your team, or your league. Our goal is simple: Make sure the kids have enough fun that they want to play again next year.

In house leagues, ice time is precious. We focus on three core pillars for every session:

  • Relentless Effort: Give your best every single shift.
  • Active Learning: Listen, engage, and never hesitate to ask "why."
  • Team First: Support your teammates on and off the ice.

We hope this makes your coaching journey more rewarding, translating into a better experience for every young player on your roster.
