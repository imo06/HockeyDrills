USER GUIDE

Mastering the Drill & Practice Labs

Drills Lab Canvas

1

Set Your Rink

Use the ◑ Half Rink toggle in the right sidebar to switch between full-ice and zone-specific drills.

2

Draw & Place

Select tools and icons from the right toolbar. Use the Select (⌖) tool to move icons, or use the Line and Path tools to map out player movement and puck flow.

3

Describe & Keys

Write the drill descriptions and use the TAGS to categorize drills and search for them later.

4

Save Your Work

Press Ctrl + S (or Cmd + S) to save to the cloud immediately. Use JSON ↓ to download a backup of your drill file.

Practice Lab

1

Build Your Drill Sequence

Search for saved drills in the sidebar and click Add to move them into your 60-minute timeline. Use the Quick Drill to add drills without pictures.

2

Adjust Timing

Update the duration for each drill. The generator will track your total practice time to ensure you stay within your ice slot.

3

Share with Coaches

Use the Share button to generate a link or export a summary for your assistant coaches and players.

4

Export to PDF

Use the PDF ↓ button to download your practice as a PDF, ready to be used on-ice!
